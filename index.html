Who is BTS?

BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean K-pop boy group formed by Big Hit Entertainment. They debuted on June 12, 2013 with the song "No More Dream" from their first album 2 Cool 4 Skool.

The group's name, BTS, is an acronym for the Korean expression Bangtan Sonyeondan (방탄소년단), literally meaning "Bulletproof Boy Scouts". The name was conceptualized with the thought that BTS would block out stereotypes, criticisms, and expectations that aim on adolescents like bullets and protect the values and ideals of today’s adolescents.
